What Awaits

Game Features

🗺️

Procedural Encounters

Every run presents new challenges, situations and enemies for the heroes! Ensuring no two battles feel the same.

⚔️

Gachapon Summoning System

Summon rare and powerful hero cards through a claw machine themed gacha system!

📜

Collection Gallery

Admire every card you unlocked in a beautifully designed gallery showcasing the hero’s lore, artwork and rarity

🔮

Strategic Card Combat

Experience deep tactical battles where positioning, speed, and decision-making determine the outcome.

🏰

Unique Heroes & Creatures

Collect powerful champions, mystical beasts, and legendary warrior, each with their own abilities, stats, and roles in battle.

🎵

Progressive Difficulty & Boss Battles

Face increasingly difficult encounters such as powerful heroes and bosses that test your strategy and deck composition
