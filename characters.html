Brindle the Boar

"The forest remembers where the road used to bend. I remember, too."

Brindle was not always a guardian. He was born in a thicket now paved over for a Radiant Kingdom outpost. He is not clever. He is not magical. But Brindle remembers everything — every tree felled, every path rerouted, every fence erected where a stream once flowed.

Origin The Shifting Woods
Affiliation Verdant Wilds (Outer Guard)
