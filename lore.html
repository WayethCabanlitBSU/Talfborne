The World of Taflborne

In the Vale of Luminara, no story stands alone. Every creature, every knight, every forgotten thing is a thread in a vast tapestry, pull one, and others tremble. What follows are not isolated biographies but interconnected chronicles, where each entry reveals bonds, rivalries, debts, and destinies shared across factions and forms.

The war has not yet begun in earnest, but the threads are tightening.

✦   ─   ✦   ─   ✦

THE THREADS TIGHTEN

Every character in Luminara carries connections—visible and invisible, acknowledged and denied. The boar knows the mushroom. The skeleton knows the knight. The witch studies the slime. The dragon watches everyone.

The war has not begun. But the threads are tightening.

When the first blow finally falls—whether struck by Kingdom, Wilds, or Beyond—the tapestry will tear. And everyone, from the lowest slime to the highest dragon, will have to choose.

Not good vs evil.

Not right vs wrong.

Not good vs evil.

But what kind of world they want Luminara to become.
